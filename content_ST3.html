Diseases and Illnesses

Diseases and illnesses are an unavoidable and prevalent part of everyones lives. Here, we will go into more details about some of the different diseases and illnesses that plague us.

Malaria

  • Malaria is one of the most deadly diseases, having caused an estimated 600,000 deaths per year.
  • Malaria is a parasite, mainly spread by female mosquitos when they feed on a human's blood.
  • Symptoms can include fevers, chills, headaches and in more severe cases, fatigue, confusion, seizures and breathing difficulties.
  • Malaria mainly occurs in warmer and poorer areas such as South America, Africa and South Asia.
  • NHS page on Malaria

Covid-19

  • Covid-19 is an incredibly contagious disease that caused the UK to go into lockdown between 2020 and 2022.
  • Covid-19 can be spread through droplets from when people coughs, speaks, sneezes etc.
  • Symptoms can include fevers, coughing, changes to smell and taste, fatigue, sore thorat and more.
  • Covid was common everywhere because of how contagious it is, but vaccines have lowered the amount of extreme cases.
  • NHS page on Covid-19
